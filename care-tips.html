How often should I wash my wig?

No, regular hair products can damage synthetic wigs. Use products specifically designed for wigs to keep them looking their best.

How do I prevent tangling?

Detangle regularly with a wide-tooth comb, store properly on a wig stand, and avoid sleeping or showering in your wig.
